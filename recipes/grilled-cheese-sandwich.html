Grilled cheese sandwich

Description

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Sliced cheese
  • Butter or mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.

  2. Step

    3. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).

  3. Step

    4. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.

  4. Step

    5. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
